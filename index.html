Who's that girl?
Hello!

I'm Kalina Atanasova, creative junkie with a curious mind, originally from Bulgaria but currently based in the Netherlands.

From a very young age, my passion for arts, crafts, and all things creative was simply undeniable. My interest in programming, however, came several years later, after I finished high school and decided to follow Chemistry studies in France. During my first semester, I enrolled in an elective programming course where I first experienced the joy of telling a computer what to do. As the weeks went by, I found myself spending more and more time coding rather than nurturing my bonds with Chemistry. A few months later, it was clear that I needed to stop fooling myself. It was then that I decided to leave the country of love and cheese to become a full-time nerd somewhere else. A place where my mediocre French pronunciation was not an issue. And here I am - 2 years later - walking around that flat earthed country half-way through my IT degree.
